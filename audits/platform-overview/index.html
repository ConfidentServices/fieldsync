The Solution

The Participation Economy

DexFuel is the infrastructure layer for a new category — a market structure where verified daily action creates measurable economic value that flows back to the people who create it.

Participate  →  Earn Joules  →  Qualify for Estimated Daily Rewards

Every verified action on DexFuel earns Joules — participation units that determine your estimated share of the daily reward pool. The more you contribute, the larger your estimated share.

This isn't passive income. It isn't staking. It isn't speculation. It's a system that measures participation and distributes estimated rewards accordingly.

How It Works

The daily participation cycle

The Daily Cycle

  1. 1

    Reward pool opens — funded by platform trading fees, ad revenue, and creator campaign deposits

  2. 2

    Participants complete verified tasks — video, social, education, buy-in, Grid participation

  3. 3

    Joules accumulate — each verified action credits Joules proportional to task value

  4. 4

    Estimated reward allocated — at cycle close, qualified participants receive their estimated share based on Joule percentage

  5. 5

    Cycle resets — 24 hours, every day

Joules Explained

Joules are participation units. They are not money. They are not tokens. They are not tradable. They have no monetary value. They measure your contribution to the platform in a given cycle and determine your estimated reward share — which is variable and not guaranteed.

Tasks

  • Video tasks — watch creator content or sponsored brand content
  • Social tasks — post, share, or comment (verified via connected social account)
  • Education tasks — complete quizzes or tutorials about a project
  • Participation tasks — buy into a launch, join a Grid, complete a referral
  • Creator tasks — custom actions defined by individual channel creators

Grids

Coordinated communities working toward collective participation targets. Grids that hit their thresholds earn estimated bonus rewards distributed among members. All Grid rewards are variable and not guaranteed.
